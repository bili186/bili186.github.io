Research Overview

This academic research focuses on the interpretability of multimodal person re-identification models. As the first author, I completed the theoretical research, experimental design and paper writing.

The research reveals the feature selection and decision-making mechanism of deep learning models in ReID tasks, providing a theoretical basis for the application and optimization of multimodal models.
